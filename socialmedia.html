Social Media Functions in UiTM

Social media plays a vital role in connecting students, faculty, and the public to Universiti Teknologi MARA, Kampus Puncak Perdana. Here are the key functions:

  • Communication: Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter allow UiTM to share announcements, updates, and news efficiently with the entire university community.
  • Promoting Events: Social media is used to publicize events such as seminars, cultural activities, and career fairs to ensure maximum participation.
  • Engaging with Students: Students can interact with the university through comments, messages, and polls, fostering a sense of community.
  • Highlighting Achievements: UiTM uses social media to showcase students’ and staff’s accomplishments, boosting morale and visibility.
  • Recruitment and Admissions: Social media serves as a tool for prospective students to learn about courses, admission processes, and campus life.

Social media not only enhances connectivity but also strengthens UiTM's reputation as a forward-thinking institution.
