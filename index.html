About UiTM Puncak Perdana

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kampus Puncak Perdana, located in Shah Alam, Malaysia, is a distinguished educational institution offering a wide array of academic programs. The campus is well-equipped with modern facilities designed to nurture students' academic, personal, and professional growth.

The campus provides a conducive learning environment, with access to various resources, including libraries, computer labs, and recreational spaces. UiTM Puncak Perdana is committed to producing graduates who are not only knowledgeable but also well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of the professional world.
